• Different types of apples and their best usage:

    • Apple
      • Cameo
        • Well suited for fresh and cooked preparations. Examples:

          1. Apple Pie
            • Classic dessert with a buttery crust and spiced filling.

          2. Apple Crumble or Crisp
            • Served warm with a crunchy oat topping.

          3. Apple Sauce
            • Served warm with a crunchy oat topping.


      • Gala
        • Popular choice for their natural sweetness, mild flavor, and juicy, crisp texture. Examples:

          1. Smoothies
            • A naturally sweet and refreshing addition to fruit smoothies, blending well with berries, bananas, and yogurt.

          2. Fruit Salads
            • Adds a crisp, juicy element to fruit salads, pairing well with ingredients like grapes, strawberries, and mint.

          3. Snacking
            • Perfect for fresh eating due to its sweet flavor and thin skin, making it easy to enjoy without peeling.


      • Golden Delicious
        • Well-suited for both raw and cooked dishes. Examples:

          1. Baking (Pies, Tarts, and Crisps)
            • Retains its shape and adds a honeyed sweetness to baked goods like pies and crisps. Balances well with spices like cinnamon and nutmeg.

          2. Sauces and Apple Butter
            • Ideal for smooth, rich sauces and apple butter, bringing a natural sweetness and requiring little added sugar.

          3. Jams
            • Works well in jams and preserves due to its balanced sweetness and ability to pair well with other fruits like strawberries or blackberries.
